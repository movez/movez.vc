Cookies Overview

A “cookie” is a small text file that is stored on your browser or device by websites, apps, online media, and advertisements that are used to remember your browser or device during and across the website visit(s).

There are two broad categories of cookies:

  • First party cookies, served directly by us to your computer or mobile device.
  • Third party cookies, which are served by a third party on our behalf. We use third party cookies for marketing and analytical purposes. The third-party cookies are outside of our control. The third parties may, at any time, change their terms of service, purpose and use of cookies, etc.

Cookies can remain on your computer or mobile device for different periods of time. Some cookies are “session cookies”, meaning that they exist only while your browser is open. These are deleted automatically once you close your browser. Other cookies are “permanent cookies”, meaning that they survive after your browser is closed. They can be used by websites to recognize your computer when you open your browser and browse the Internet again.

We may use cookies for several purposes, including:

  • analysing website’s traffic;
  • monitor the performance of our website and to continually improve it;
  • customize and enhance your online experience;
  • ensure your privacy is protected;
  • evaluate our websites’ advertising and promotional effectiveness;
  • generally understand the online behaviour and interests of people who interact with the website.

For privacy and cookie information relating to third party services, please consult their privacy and cookie policies respectively.
