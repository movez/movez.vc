Joshua Hollis

Managing Partner

Joshua Hollis is a highly driven and passionate software entrepreneur with a keen eye for software intricacies. His peers commend his ability to pinpoint inconspicuous system flaws and ingeniously devise solutions with savvy UI/UX. A tech enthusiast at heart, he built his first computer at the age of 9. In 2019, he earned his degree in International Business Management and Computer Science from Boston University. Joshua is the founder of Movez, a Techstars JP Morgan-backed SaaS company, an innovative Experience Management and Event Discovery SaaS.

Jameson Hollis

Chief Investment Officer

Jameson Hollis, a seasoned entrepreneur with licenses including Series 6TO, Series 7TO, Series 26, Series 63, and SIE, excels in corporate financial planning. As the Managing Partner of Modern Woodmen of America, he has showcased his leadership in the financial sector. Previously, he leveraged his expertise in investment planning at UBS and as an Investment Products Coordinator at John Hancock. A Posse Scholar and Boston University graduate, he is also a two-time founder, highlighting his entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to innovation.

Edmond Curtin

General Partner

Edmond Curtin, a prominent legal professional, currently serves as the Founding Partner of Lawcabinet Partnership LLP in London, where he has excelled for over 12 years. Lawcabinet Partnership LLP stands as a testament to his legal prowess and entrepreneurial spirit. Prior to this, Edmond served as Special Counsel at Cadwalader, Wickersham & Taft LLP in London for two years, contributing his expertise to the Capital Markets department. His extensive career also includes a significant role as Managing Director at Credit Suisse, where he dedicated nearly a decade to the financial industry. Additionally, Edmond briefly assumed the position of Interim CEO at Reyl London, demonstrating his adaptability and leadership in various professional settings.

Henry Malkasian

Portfolio Manager

Henry Malkasian is a seasoned finance professional with a BA in English from Skidmore College and an LLM in Venture Capital with Distinction from the University of Westminster, London. His impressive career spans positions at Shearson/American Express in trading, Senior VP at W R Grace & Co., and Senior VP at GRACE Healthcare, reflecting his extensive expertise in finance and investment, including roles in Block Trading, Options Trading, and Equity Trading desks.
