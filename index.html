Chairman of Board

Joshua Hollis is the Founder and Managing Partner of Movez Ventures, where he leads investments in artificial intelligence infrastructure, robotics, enterprise software, and emerging technologies. A software entrepreneur with over a decade of product development experience, Joshua founded Movez, a Techstars J.P. Morgan-backed technology company, leading product strategy, software engineering, and commercialization.

Joshua graduated from Boston University with a degree in International Business Management. A lifelong technologist, he built his first computer at age nine and has since designed high-performance AI computing systems. An early adopter of Bitcoin and blockchain technology, he has long been focused on transformative technologies shaping the future economy. Joshua also earned first place in a Google marketing competition, outperforming more than 120 participants. His investment philosophy centers on backing the infrastructure powering the future of intelligence, including compute, semiconductors, robotics, energy, and AI-native enterprise software.

Jameson Hollis serves as Chief Investment Officer of Movez Ventures, bringing extensive experience across wealth management, investment products, financial planning, and capital markets. His background spans both institutional finance and entrepreneurship, providing a disciplined framework for evaluating investment opportunities and supporting portfolio companies through growth. Jameson has held leadership positions with Modern Woodmen of America, UBS, and John Hancock, where he advised clients on investment strategies, retirement planning, and complex financial products. He holds FINRA licenses including the Series 6, Series 7, Series 26, Series 63, and Securities Industry Essentials (SIE), reflecting a comprehensive understanding of securities markets, regulatory compliance, and portfolio management. A Posse Scholar and Boston University graduate, Jameson is also a two-time entrepreneur with a passion for identifying exceptional founders and helping businesses scale responsibly. At Movez Ventures, he oversees investment diligence, portfolio construction, and capital allocation while helping shape the firm's long-term investment strategy across artificial intelligence, enterprise software, robotics, and financial technology.

Edmond Curtin serves as Chairman of Movez Ventures and brings more than three decades of international leadership experience spanning law, investment banking, financial services, and corporate governance. His career has placed him at the intersection of global capital markets, complex transactions, and strategic advisory for multinational institutions. Edmond is the Founding Partner of Lawcabinet Partnership LLP in London, where he advises clients on corporate, financial, and cross-border legal matters. Prior to founding the firm, he served as Special Counsel at Cadwalader, Wickersham & Taft LLP and spent nearly a decade as Managing Director at Credit Suisse, advising on sophisticated capital markets transactions. He also served as Interim CEO of REYL London, further demonstrating his leadership across both legal and financial institutions. As Chairman of Movez Ventures, Edmond provides strategic oversight, governance, and institutional perspective to the firm. His experience advising global financial institutions and high-growth companies strengthens Movez Ventures' ability to support ambitious founders building technologies that will define the future economy.

Investment Focus

We invest across the physical and digital infrastructure required for artificial intelligence to scale.

Compute & Semiconductors

AI accelerators, advanced chips, high-bandwidth memory, packaging, and networking.

Energy & Data Centers

Power generation, cooling, data-center systems, and infrastructure for AI factories.

Robotics & Physical Systems

Robotics, autonomy, bearings, precision motion, sensors, and advanced materials.

AI Applications & Fintech

Intelligent software and financial platforms built on next-generation AI infrastructure.

Investment Thesis

Artificial intelligence represents one of the most significant technological transformations in history. We believe the greatest opportunities will emerge from the infrastructure enabling intelligence itself—from compute, semiconductors, networking, and energy to robotics, intelligent software, and financial infrastructure. Our investment thesis outlines the long-term trends shaping the future of the AI economy.
